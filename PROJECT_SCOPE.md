Traffic Accident Risk Prediction & Road Safety Analytics

Business Problem
Urban road accidents cause significant loss of life and economic damage.
City authorities need a data-driven system to:
- Identify high-risk accident zones
- Understand factors contributing to accident severity
- Support infrastructure and policy decisions

Objective
Build an analytics and machine learning system using open government data to:
- Analyze accident patterns
- Perform statistical risk analysis
- Predict accident severity using machine learning

Stakeholders
- Traffic police departments
- Urban planning authorities
- Smart city governance teams
